**Pre-Owned Car Pricing & Market Value Analysis**

An end-to-end data analytics project evaluating pre-owned vehicle market values, pricing distributions, and depreciation trends across popular sedan models (including Audi, Acura, and competing luxury/midsize brands).

 **Executive Summary**
Understanding vehicle valuation dynamics in the pre-owned market requires analyzing multiple interconnected variables—such as mileage, trim level, model year, and historical market demand. 
This project cleans, models, and visualizes pre-owned sedan market data to identify fair market pricing, evaluate price depreciation curves, and highlight optimal value-for-money purchasing thresholds for buyers and analysts.

**Key Objectives**
 **Market Price Benchmarking:** Quantify average trading prices, variance, and feature premium impact across target models.
 **Depreciation Analysis:** Model value retention relative to vehicle age and accumulated mileage.
 **Outlier & Value Detection:** Build a structured framework to flag underpriced listings and statistical pricing anomalies.
 **Interactive Reporting:** Deliver clear, actionable dashboards and data visualizations for stakeholders.

**Key Insights & Findings**
**Mileage vs. Price Correlation:** Identified critical mileage thresholds where vehicle value drops most sharply.
**Trim Level Premiums:** Evaluated the secondary market value retention of higher-tier trim packages vs. base models.
**Comparative Brand Value:** Analyzed price elasticity and depreciation differences across luxury sedan competitors.
